Detailed Description

This operator performs fitness top-scaling. Given f, a fraction of individuals. Best individuals are given a score 1/m*n, where n is the number of individuals. Alternatively, a number q of individuals can be provided, instead of fraction f. The others are given 0.

TopScaling is compatible with multi-objective optimization.
